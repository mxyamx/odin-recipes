tacos


Here is a recipe to make delicious tacos in less than half an hour! You can adjust the portions to accomodate your needs.Spice up your dinner with your family or friends with a quick and simple recipe!

Ingredient

  • ground beef
  • Salsa of your choice
  • taco shells
  • lettuce
  • Shredded cheese of your choice

Steps to follow:

  1. Cook beef on medium heat for around 10 minutes
  2. Add salsa while cooking the beef for around 5 minutes
  3. Heat taco shells(follow package instructions)
  4. Add the ground beef and salsa mixture into a heated taco shell
  5. Add shredded cheese and lettuce in the taco
  6. Enjoy with family and friends!
