Burger


Quick and easy recipe to make a delicious burger for the whole family to enjoy.All you need is a few ingredients,25 minutes and you have a delicious meal!

Ingredients

  • Chicken stripes
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Shredded cheese
  • Ranch sauce
  • Burger buns

Steps to follow:

  1. Cook the chicken stripes on medium heat for around ten minutes
  2. Heat the burger buns in the oven until you see that they are toasted
  3. Add Ranch sauce on the buns
  4. Place one chicken stripe on top of the sauce
  5. Add lettuce,tomatoes and cheese to the burger
  6. Enjoy with your family and friends!
