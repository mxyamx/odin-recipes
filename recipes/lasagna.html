lasagna


This is a very simple recipe to make delicious lasagna. It should not take much time and you can change the portions to accomodate your needs.

Ingredients

  • tomato sauce
  • ground beef
  • olive oil
  • onion
  • italian seasoning
  • milk
  • shredded mozzarella
  • egg
  • parmesan

Steps to follow:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. cook lasagna noodles
  3. Add ground beef and onion in a pan with olive oil and heat at medium high heat
  4. Mix in another bowl half a cup of mozzarella and the egg
  5. Spread one cup of tomato on the bottom of a baking dish(size is up to you).Add three lasagna noodles and the cheese mixture.Spread tomato sauce on top to finish the first layer.
  6. Repeat the last step to make three layers.
  7. Put the baking dish in the oven and bake for 40 minutes.
  8. Wait for it to cool down and enjoy your lasagna!
